“A tribute to culture, craft, and community”

Born from Love & Legacy

Founded in 2023 by Vanessa and Pedro Leon, Maria Mezcaleria is a tribute to Vanessa's love for mezcal and a desire to bring something special to the Waco community.

Named after the iconic Mexican actress María Félix — known as "La Doña" — the name combines her legacy with the word Mezcaleria, perfectly capturing the blend of culture and craft that defines who we are.

With executive chef Arturo Leon at the helm, every dish is crafted with passion and authenticity — from the rich Michoacán-style carnitas to our signature Queso Flameado with Oaxacan cheese.
